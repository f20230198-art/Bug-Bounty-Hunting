# Simple Sign-Up Guide (Step by Step)

> This guide is written for complete beginners. Follow one step at a time.

[← Back to Home](./README.md)

---

## What You Need First

1. A working email address that you can open right now
2. A browser (Chrome or Firefox)
3. 15 to 20 minutes

---

## Accounts You Must Create

| Platform | Sign-up Link | Why You Need It |
|----------|--------------|-----------------|
| PortSwigger Academy | [Sign Up](https://portswigger.net/web-security) | Workshop labs |
| TryHackMe | [Sign Up](https://tryhackme.com/signup) | Extra practice |

---

## 1) PortSwigger Academy

1. Open: [https://portswigger.net/web-security](https://portswigger.net/web-security)
2. Click `Sign in` or `Register`.
3. Enter email and password.
4. Open your email inbox.
5. Find the verification email from PortSwigger and click the verification link.
6. Log in again.
7. Open any lab page and check that you can click `Access the lab`.

If you can open a lab, this account is done.

---

## 2) TryHackMe

1. Open: [https://tryhackme.com/signup](https://tryhackme.com/signup)
2. Create username, email, and password.
3. Complete the captcha.
4. Open your email inbox.
5. Click the verification link from TryHackMe.
6. Log in.
7. Open the dashboard and confirm your account is active.

If you can log in and see your dashboard, this account is done.

---

## 3) HackerOne

1. Open: [https://hackerone.com/users/sign_up](https://hackerone.com/users/sign_up)
2. Select `Hacker` profile.
3. Enter your details and create password.
4. Verify your email from inbox.
5. Log in and open your profile settings.
6. Add basic profile details (username, country, short bio).

If you can log in and edit your profile, this account is done.

## Quick Fixes (If Something Breaks)

### "I did not get the email"

1. Check spam/junk folder.
2. Wait 2 to 3 minutes.
3. Click `Resend verification email`.
4. Try using Gmail if school email blocks security mails.

### "Username already taken"

Add a number at the end. Example: `alex1234`.

### "Captcha keeps failing"

Refresh page once and try again slowly. Disable ad blocker for that page.

### "Page not opening on college Wi-Fi"

Try mobile hotspot for sign-up, then log in later from Wi-Fi.
